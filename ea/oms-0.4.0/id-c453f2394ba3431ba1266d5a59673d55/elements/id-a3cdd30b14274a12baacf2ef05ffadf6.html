Recommendations for support during warranty and maintenance • PEA to consider similar requirement for restauration and resolution time as ADMS project: 12. Warranty 12.1 The supplier shall guarantee the proper functioning of the Equipment for period of Thirty-Six months after passed Provisional Acceptance Certificate (PAC) (Warranty Period). Supplier undertakes to remedy the defects resulting from bad bad material or poor workmanship for entire Warranty Period and for that period only. 12.2 In order to be able to avail himself of its right under this article, PSP shall notify Supplier in writing without delay of any defect that has appeared an shall allow Supplier full opportunity to inspec and remedy it. 12.3 Upon receipt of such notification, Supplier shall take the following action: a) In case where SCADA Function is out of order, Supplier shall complete the inspection and correction or work-around within 4 (four) hours after formal notification by PSP. b) //// In case where Power System Application is out of order, Supplier shall complete the inspection and correction or work-around within 24 (twenty-four) hours after forml notification by PSP. c) In case of other breakdown apart from SCADA or Power System Application, excluding resale products, including 3rd party hardware ad software, Supplier shall complete the inspection and correction or work-around within 72 (seventy-two) hours after formal notification by PSP, or provide the Resolution plan how and when the issue will be resolved.
