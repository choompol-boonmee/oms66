2. TDMS Backup Concept ()
2. TDMS Backup Concept The TDMS hardware and software at each new data center shall be a replica (mirror image) of the hardware and software at the other data center. To support critical TDMS functionality, application servers at each data center shall be redundant. Moreover, Dispatchers at the control centers shall be provided with bump-less access to critical SCADA, EMS, or DMS applications in the event any of these applications can no longer execute on any of the redundant servers in which they are installed at one data center and must therefore execute on one of the corresponding redundant servers at the other data center. This backup/failover feature shall also apply to other critical functions and processes requiring unlimited access to system resources. The way in which the Contractor’s solution meets the backup/failover feature shall have been clearly described in the Contractor’s proposal and shall be demonstrated during system implementation. Demonstration shall include the worst-case disaster recovery scenario in which all facilities at one of the data centers become unavailable.
